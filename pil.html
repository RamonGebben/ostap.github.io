Ilse Boer

Owner of Public Image Labels on the Shoutenstraat, Utrecht

"We always try to offer a very personal feeling when people visit the shop. Usually they leave with something totally unexpected in a good way, and that’s something they don’t tend to do with online shopping"

I step into the fresh and elegant show room while Ilse gives one-on-one fashion assistance to one of her regulars. I watch for a few minutes while they consider a new dress in the mirror before im invited to perch on one of the antique arm cheers towards the rear of the homely space.

What style tips do you have this year?

"The style is classical with a twist. I have one belgian label for the women, an Italian one for men, and one french, one german for both men and women. I find the best at fairs in Paris and Berlin, for example some organised by Bread And Butter.

"My favourite item at the moment is the combination of these green machine-washed lamp-leather and a simple blouse on the top, its very elegant. But for the men, this season the safari jackets are well in fashion.

What inspired you to start P.I.L?

"I studied fashion in the academy of art in Utrecht then for 15 years I modeled, then wholesale but once I had my son I wanted to be able to cycle to work so I joined Willem at Daen's. He wanted a women’s line so I helped with that.

"I liked the name of an English punk band called Public Image Ltd, so thats how I got to Public Image Label. I don't even like punk music really, but they had a great brand.

What is it like for clients when they spend time here?

"We always try to offer a very personal feeling when people visit the shop. Usually they leave with something totally unexpected in a good way, and that’s something they don’t tend to do with online shopping.

Here it’s all about impulses and to get inspired, while also being able to feel the fit of the clothes.

We also try to help people quickly so they can be in and out if they need more convenience. I really like that you can make your own atmosphere in the shop as well, and here we are very refined.
